The Best Movies according to Chetang

My top 3 movies of all time

  1. Kal Ho Na Ho

    2. Aman inserts positivity and liveliness into the life of Naina, a defeatist MBA student with familial problems. Naina, smitten with him, wishes to wed him, but is unaware of his terminal illness.

  2. Rehna Hai Tere Dil Mein

    3. Maddy impersonates Rajeev, the person his love interest, Reena, is about to marry and attempts to woo her. However, he is unprepared for the moment when the truth will be revealed.

  3. Dil Chahta Hai

    4. Three close friends are separated after college due to their different approaches towards relationships. Akash goes to Australia, Sameer gets busy wooing a girl and Siddharth devotes himself to art.
